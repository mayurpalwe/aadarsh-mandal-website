About Us

Aadarsh Ganesh & Navdurga Mandal, Anturli — Established 1993 Our journey began in 1993, when a few enthusiastic youths of Anturli decided to bring home a small Ganesh murti with great devotion but limited resources. What started as a humble celebration with a simple idol, homemade decorations, and heartfelt prayers slowly grew year by year into a grand festival that unites our entire community. As the years passed, the size of the murti grew, the decorations became more creative, and the participation of devotees increased. Soon, our Mandal also began celebrating Navratri Utsav, bringing together families and youngsters for Garba, Dandiya, and cultural events that became the heartbeat of our village evenings. Today, Aadarsh Ganesh Mandal & Aadarsh Navdurga Mandal is not only known for devotion and celebration but also for its social work, cultural programs, and community service. From a small murti in 1993 to a grand and divine celebration today, our Mandal is a living example of how faith, unity, and tradition can grow stronger with every generation.
